# Hormozi Response Style — Examples

## Example 1: CTA Comment Reply

**Post topic**: "5 ways to automate your sales pipeline"
**CTA word**: "guide"
**Comment**: "This is great! Do you have a guide for this?"
**Commenter**: Sarah Mueller, Sales Lead at TechCorp

**Response:**
```
Sarah, here you go: [link]

Start with section 3 (the follow-up cadence) — that's the one thing that moved the needle fastest for the sales teams I've worked with.

Let me know if you have questions.
```

---

## Example 2: General Comment Reply

**Post topic**: "Why most LinkedIn outreach fails"
**Comment**: "So true, I get 10 of these a day and delete them all"
**Commenter**: Marco Bianchi, Founder at DataFlow

**Response:**
```
Marco, that's exactly the problem — most outreach is about the sender, not the reader.

The ones that actually get replies? They reference something specific about you and lead with a question, not a pitch.
```

---

## Example 3: Connection Request (2nd Degree)

**Context**: Commented on your post about hiring, 2nd degree connection
**Their profile**: Head of People at a 50-person startup

**Connection request:**
```
Lisa, saw your take on the hiring post — solid point about culture fit vs. culture add.

I write about scaling teams without losing quality. Figured it might be relevant given what you're building at [company].

Happy to connect.
```

---

## Example 4: Post-Connection DM

**Context**: Connection accepted, they're a marketing director

**Message:**
```
Thanks for connecting, Jan.

Quick one: What's the biggest bottleneck you're seeing in your lead gen right now?

No pitch — I'm putting together content on this topic and want to cover what actually matters to people in marketing leadership.
```

---

## Example 5: 5-Day Follow-Up

**Context**: Sent a DM 5 days ago, no reply, they're a CTO

**Message:**
```
Thomas, just bumping this.

Saw this case study yesterday about a CTO who cut deployment time by 60% with a surprisingly simple change — thought you might find it interesting: [link]

Worth 3 minutes if you're looking at DevOps efficiency.
```

---

## Example 6: Re-engagement

**Context**: Had a conversation 2 weeks ago about AI automation, went silent

**Message:**
```
Anna, just saw the new EU AI Act updates this morning.

Made me think of our conversation about compliance in your automation stack.

Are you adjusting anything on your end, or waiting to see how it plays out?
```
